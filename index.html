OUR MODEL

Trained. Not guessed.

We didn't prompt a generic AI. We built something purpose-made for talent.

We generated over 50,000 synthetic CVs per job role — built to represent the full spectrum of candidates competing for each position globally. Our model was trained on this data to understand what genuinely separates candidates at different percentile levels for each specific role.

The result is a scoring engine that reflects how real hiring works — not a generic checklist. Your percentile is a calibrated signal, not a guess.

Separate model per role — quant analyst scoring is entirely different from product manager scoring
Built for global candidates — the model accounts for experience and achievement across all geographies
Cohort-relative — your percentile is always within your experience tier, never distorted by seniority
